Instructions

Objective

Capture all opponent pieces. Moves are determined by throwing the four sticks.

Each stick has two sides: bright side and dark side.

After throwing, count how many bright sides are facing up. That number is your move value (1–4, or 6 if all are dark).

  • 1, 4, or 6 → Play again after your move.
  • 2 or 3 → Turn passes to your opponent.
Example: 2 bright → value 2

Your Turn

  1. Press Throw Sticks.
  2. Select one of your pieces with highlights.
  3. Click a highlighted destination to move.

First move for each piece usually requires a 1.

Captures & Rows

  • Landing on an opponent captures it.
  • You can’t re-enter your own start row.
  • The last row becomes available only after all your pieces have left the start row.
